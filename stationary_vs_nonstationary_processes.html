T = 200 observations
Process 1 — Stationary
AR(1): Mean-reverting
y_t = 0.7·y_{t-1} + ε_t, ε_t ~ iid N(0,1)
Constant mean Constant variance Decaying autocorrelation Forecastable long-run level
Process 2 — Non-stationary (unit root)
Random Walk: I(1)
y_t = y_{t-1} + ε_t, ε_t ~ iid N(0,1)
Variance → ∞ with T No mean reversion Persistent shocks Difference to make stationary
Process 3 — White Noise
iid Noise: Unpredictable baseline
y_t = ε_t, ε_t ~ iid N(0,1)
Constant mean (= 0) Constant variance Zero autocorrelation No forecastability (E[y_{t+1}] = 0)
